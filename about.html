About

Sounding Line Economics brings together applied economics, risk analysis, and decision-focused modeling to help clients evaluate policy choices, quantify impacts, and make better decisions under uncertainty.

The firm specializes in analytically rigorous, policy-relevant work at the intersection of economics, risk, environment, and public decision-making.

Particular areas of expertise include environmental risk, extreme events, infrastructure disruption, and the economic value of information.

Sounding Line Economics is built to support clients who need clear, credible analysis rather than generic consulting or off-the-shelf reporting.
